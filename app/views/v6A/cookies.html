What are cookies?

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

They store information about how you use the website, such as the pages you visit.

Cookies are not viruses or computer programmes. They are very small so do not take up much space.

How we use cookies

We only use cookies that are needed to make the exception visualisation work, for example by keeping it secure.

We do not use any other type of cookies.
